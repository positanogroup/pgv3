№ 004 — Operating Model

How a Positano engagement actually starts.

WK 0

Discover

A 30-minute call. We map the moment, the risks, the decisions stacking up. No deck pitched, no commitment asked.

Two-week diagnostic. We get into the GL, the model, the data room. We surface what to fix, defer, or kill.

Written scope. Cadence. Deliverables. Outcomes you can hold us to. Approved before any retainer starts.
