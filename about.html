Positano Group is a strategic finance practice for founders and investors at the inflection points — fundraises, transitions, transactions, and the long quiet stretches where the right model changes everything.

Fifteen years inside the institutions every operating CFO eventually calls — McKinsey, Evercore, a Fortune 500 finance org, two PE-backed operating roles. The pattern that recurred: founders at $5–50M revenue need senior finance judgment weekly, but can't justify a permanent CFO hire until Series B or C.

Positano Group exists to fill that seat — the institutional finance partner a founder would hire if they could find one. Rigor where it matters, hands dirty when needed, strategic vision throughout.
